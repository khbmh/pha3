What types of housing do you offer?

We provide a variety of housing options including apartments, single-family homes, townhouses, and condos. For details on available properties, visit our website or contact our office.

What are the requirements for renting a property?

To rent, you typically need a good credit score, stable income (usually 2.5 to 3 times the rent), and a positive rental history. A background check may also be required.

How do I request maintenance or repairs?

Submit maintenance requests through our online portal or call our maintenance team. For emergencies, use our 24/7 emergency contact number.
